Our Services and Features

We offer real-time translation from English/Hindi to Indian Sign Language and vice versa. Our platform is powered by AI technology and a 3D avatar for immersive, human-like sign language translation. Key features include:

  • Real-time translation of audio, text, and video.
  • Multi-platform support for desktop and mobile devices.
  • Interactive 3D avatar performing ISL gestures.
  • User-friendly interface with audio/video input controls.
  • Customizable settings for language preferences.

How to Use It

Our platform is designed to be intuitive. Follow these simple steps:

  • Select your input method: audio, text, or video.
  • Enter or speak your message.
  • Watch as the message is translated into ISL by our 3D avatar.
  • Adjust settings as needed for language preferences.
